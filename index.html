The Rajshahi City Corporation Employment Skill Development Institute (RCCESDI), is a visionary initiative spearheaded by A. H. M. Khairuzzaman Liton, the Honorable Mayor of Rajshahi City Corporation. Committed to empowering young professionals, the institute focuses on developing IT and Freelancing skills. With a dedicated team of trainers and state-of-the-art facilities, RCCESDI ensures a hands-on learning experience.

Honorable Chairman

In an era defined by rapid technological advancements and dynamic job markets, our institute stands as a beacon of opportunity and transformation. We are committed to empowering the youth with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of employment, with a special focus on IT and freelancing.
At RCC ESDI, we believe in the potential of each individual to make a positive impact through their unique skills. Our dedicated team of educators and trainers, under the guidance of Md. Habibur Rahman, ensures that our programs are tailored to meet the demands of today's professional world.
