About Us

Welcome to the Rajshahi City Corporation Employment Skill Development Institute(RCCESDI), a visionary initiative led by A. H. M. Khairuzzaman Liton, the Mayor of Rajshahi City Corporation. Our mission is to empower the youth by providing cutting-edge skill development programs, with a special focus on the dynamic fields of the IT sector and freelancing.
At RCCESDI, we offer courses in four diverse sectors: Web Design and Development, Graphics Design, Digital Marketing, and Video Editing. Our goal is to equip students with practical skills that align with the demands of the modern job market.
Our state-of-the-art computer lab, named Joy Set Center, was made possible through the support of the Honorable Minister Zunaid Ahmed Palak. This lab is equipped with the latest technology to ensure that our students have access to a modern and conducive learning environment.
Coordinating the institute is Md. Habibur Rahman, the former Principal of Rajshahi College and a renowned figure in the field of education. His leadership adds a wealth of experience and knowledge to our institution.
Here at RCCESDI, we go beyond technical skills. Students not only learn hard skills in their chosen fields but also acquire essential soft skills and language proficiency. Our holistic approach prepares them not just for employment but for a successful and fulfilling career.
Join us in this transformative journey where education meets empowerment, and aspirations turn into achievements.
